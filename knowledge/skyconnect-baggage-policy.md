# SkyConnect Airlines — Baggage Services Guide

## Lost Baggage Policy

### Reporting a Lost Bag
- Customers should report lost baggage within 24 hours of arrival
- Reports can be filed online at skyconnectairlines.com or at the airport baggage service desk
- Each report generates a case number for tracking
- Customers receive email and SMS updates on their bag status

### Priority Levels
- **Standard**: Normal processing, estimated resolution 48–72 hours
- **Priority - Medical**: Bag contains prescription medication. Escalated handling, target resolution 24–48 hours. Customer may be eligible for emergency medication reimbursement up to $200.
- **Priority - Business**: Bag contains essential business materials. Expedited search and delivery.
- **Priority - VIP**: SkyPlatinum and SkyDiamond members receive automatic VIP priority with dedicated tracking agent.

### Bag Tracking Process
1. **Report Submitted** — Case created, confirmation sent to customer
2. **Bag Located** — Our system scans all airports in the route for the bag using the bag tag number
3. **In Transit** — Bag is routed to the customer's delivery address via SkyConnect Ground Services
4. **Out for Delivery** — Local courier dispatched
5. **Delivered** — Bag delivered, customer confirms receipt

### Typical Timelines
- Domestic flights: 24–48 hours for bag location, 48–72 hours for delivery
- International flights: 48–72 hours for bag location, 72–120 hours for delivery
- Medical priority cases: Expedited to 24–48 hours total
- 95% of lost bags are located within 48 hours
- 98% of lost bags are returned within 5 days

### Compensation and Reimbursement
- Customers may claim up to $50/day for essential items while waiting (receipts required)
- Maximum interim expense claim: $200 for domestic, $400 for international
- If a bag is not found within 21 days, it is declared lost and full compensation is provided
- Maximum liability: $3,800 per passenger for domestic flights (per DOT regulations)
- Medical expense reimbursement: Up to $200 for emergency prescription refills

## Damaged Baggage Policy

### Reporting Damage
- Damage must be reported within 7 days of receipt
- Photos of damage are required
- Original receipts for the bag are helpful but not required

### Compensation
- Repair costs covered up to the bag's depreciated value
- If unrepairable, replacement value up to $3,800
- Cosmetic damage (scratches, scuffs) from normal handling is not covered

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How do I track my lost bag?
A: You can track your bag using your case number at skyconnectairlines.com/track or by chatting with us. We also send automatic email and SMS updates at each stage.

### Q: What if my bag contains medication?
A: Cases with medication are automatically flagged as Medical Priority and receive expedited handling. Our target is 24–48 hours for resolution. You may also be eligible for up to $200 reimbursement for emergency prescription refills at a local pharmacy. Please keep your pharmacy receipt.

### Q: Can I change my delivery address?
A: Yes, you can update your delivery address at any time before the bag is out for delivery. Contact us via chat or call 1-800-SKY-HELP.

### Q: What if my bag isn't found?
A: If your bag is not located within 21 days, it will be declared lost. You will receive full compensation up to $3,800 per DOT regulations. We will work with you to document the contents and their value.

### Q: Do I need to be home for delivery?
A: We offer flexible delivery windows (morning, afternoon, evening). If no one is home, the courier will leave the bag in a secure location or attempt redelivery the next day.

### Q: What is SkyConnect's baggage allowance?
A: Economy: 1 checked bag (50 lbs). Economy Plus: 2 checked bags (50 lbs each). Business: 2 checked bags (70 lbs each). First: 3 checked bags (70 lbs each). SkyGold and above members get one additional free checked bag.

### Q: How do I file a claim for interim expenses?
A: You can submit expense receipts through your case page online or by emailing receipts@skyconnectairlines.com with your case number. Claims are processed within 5–7 business days.
